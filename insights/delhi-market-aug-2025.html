Delhi Market Update — Aug 2025

19 Aug 2025

Snapshot: selective urgency is back in Greater Kailash and Vasant Vihar. LBZ inventory is tight; sellers are confident and countering. Serious buyers are narrowing to verified, ready titles.

What moved

  • GK-II builder floors: clean, new-build 3/4BHKs with parking saw multiple calls; realistic asks are clearing.
  • Vasant Vihar D/E blocks: renovated upper floors with lift gathered quick interest; ground floors still command a premium.
  • LBZ: meaningful short-listing, but negotiations stretch longer — documentation & possession timelines decide outcomes.

What’s driving it

Verified papers, parking, and lift access remain non-negotiable. Buyers are prioritizing time-to-possession and clear MoU structures over “asking price theater”.

Our take

For sellers: pricing within the live band + clean terms is clearing quickly. For buyers: be ready with documents and a clear approval route — the best units aren’t staying public.
