Dwarka Expressway — Delhi adjacency & liquidity

  • Why buyers pick it: Airport access, Delhi proximity, deep resale pool.
  • Price journey: ~₹9.5k/sq ft (2020) ➜ ~₹18.5k/sq ft (2025).
  • Who it fits: frequent flyers, Delhi families, NRIs, investors.
  • Watchouts: higher entry cost, transaction cost; pick developer timelines carefully.

SPR — Space, value & family-first living

  • Why buyers pick it: larger formats for the price, new-gen amenities, fast-improving social infra.
  • Price today: ~₹13k/sq ft corridor average; still ~30% below Dwarka for similar sizes.
  • Who it fits: end-use families, value seekers, upgraders from central Gurgaon.
  • Watchouts: infra still rolling out by cluster; visit at peak hours to test commute.

5-step plan to choose without overwhelm

  1. Fix your non-negotiables: commute ceiling (mins), school radius, pet/elder-friendly needs.
  2. Pick a band: ₹1.5–2.0 / 2.0–2.8 / 2.8–4.0 / 4.0–6.0 / 6.0–8.0 Cr+. We’ll map matching towers.
  3. Shortlist 3 + 3: three towers on Dwarka, three on SPR; compare stack/floor/possession.
  4. Visit at peak time: test real commute; walk lobbies, lifts, club usage at 6–8 pm.
  5. Verify & lock: builder track, phase approvals, T&Cs, total cost sheet, rent comps.
