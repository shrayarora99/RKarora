South Delhi — legacy, address value, everyday convenience

  • Why people love it: tree-lined lanes, clubs, markets, top schools & hospitals within 10–15 minutes.
  • Product: independent/builder floors & bungalows; bigger rooms, custom finishes, privacy.
  • Who it fits: families valuing address & lifestyle; buyers preserving wealth in scarce micro-markets.
  • Watchouts: parking/maintenance vary by plot & age; elevator/genset depend on the building.

Gurgaon — modern amenities, organised townships, growth

  • Why people love it: gated security, clubs, pools, gyms, elevators, power-backup, on-site maintenance.
  • Product: high-rises and managed communities across Golf Course Road/Extension, SPR, Dwarka Exp.
  • Who it fits: families wanting “managed living”; professionals & NRIs; investors seeking liquidity.
  • Watchouts: choose corridor carefully; verify possession timelines and actual club density.

One-minute decision matrix

  • Address & walkable legacy life? Start with South Delhi.
  • Modern managed living & better space/₹? Start with Gurgaon.
  • Nervous about resale? Gurgaon’s branded towers generally offer deeper buyer pools.
  • Balanced plan: Live in South Delhi, invest in Gurgaon — or live in Gurgaon, keep legacy asset in South Delhi.
