FAQ

South Delhi vs Gurgaon — which is better in 2025?

South Delhi offers legacy addresses and scarce supply (floors & bungalows). Gurgaon offers modern amenities, newer stock, and more gated communities. Choice depends on lifestyle and commute. We’ll propose 3 options that fit your brief.

What does ₹1–3 Cr buy right now?

Typically mid-segment apartments on Dwarka Expressway/SPR and select Noida luxury pockets. In South Delhi, this band is limited to compact floors/fringes.
