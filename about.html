Who We Are

RK Estates was founded in 1999 by Rakesh Arora with a vision to bring trust and transparency to Delhi’s luxury real estate market. Over the years, the firm has built a reputation for integrity, verified deals, and long-term relationships with both buyers and sellers.

In recent years, Shray Arora joined the business to carry this legacy forward—bringing modern market intelligence, digital-first execution, and a client-first approach to every transaction.

Today, the advisory focuses on private submissions, serious matches, and smooth transactions across Defence Colony, Vasant Vihar, Westend, Panchsheel, Greater Kailash (GK), Lutyens Bungalow Zone (LBZ) & Gurgaon.
